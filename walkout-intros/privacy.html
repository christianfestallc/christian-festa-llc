Walkout Intros Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2026

Built with respect for your data

Walkout Intros ("we," "us," or "the App") is an iOS application developed by Christian Festa LLC.

It helps coaches, announcers, and teams create professional stadium-style walk-up intros. Users generate AI voice scripts (e.g. “Now batting, number 99, Michael Jordan”), pair them with walk-up songs from Apple Music or their library, and play full team lineups in order for games.

We collect almost nothing — and we’re transparent about the little we do.

Walkout Intros does not collect personal data, device identifiers, usage statistics, or analytics. The only data that ever leaves your device is the text you provide for AI voice generation — and even that is temporary and not stored.

Information We Collect (and What We Don’t)

  • AI Voice Generation: When you create a walk-up intro, the player name, number, custom script text, and chosen voice settings are temporarily sent to our AI service provider solely to generate the audio. This data is not stored by Christian Festa LLC or the provider after the audio is created, is never used to train models, and is deleted immediately.
  • Song Selection: Choosing songs from Apple Music or your personal library happens entirely on your device or through Apple’s secure APIs. We never see, store, or access your music library or listening history.
  • Team & Lineup Data: All teams, players, lineups, and saved intros are stored only on your device using iOS secure local storage. Nothing is uploaded or synced unless you manually enable iCloud Backup for the app.
  • In-App Purchases: Walkout Intros Pro (monthly or yearly subscriptions with a 1-week introductory offer) is processed entirely by Apple. We receive no payment details, email addresses, or purchase information.
  • Analytics / Tracking / Third Parties: We currently collect zero analytics, crash reports, or usage data. No advertising SDKs, tracking frameworks, or third-party services that would collect or transmit data are used. We may add optional analytics in the future and will clearly update this policy before doing so.

Data Storage

Everything you create — teams, players, lineups, scripts, and intros — stays on your device. Deleting the app removes all local data. There are no servers, no accounts, and no cloud storage of your content.

Sharing or Selling Data

We do not share, sell, or disclose any data. The only temporary transmission is the script text sent to our AI provider for the sole purpose of generating voice audio, as described above.

Security

Your data is protected by Apple’s sandboxed environment and secure local storage. AI requests use encrypted connections and contain only the minimal information needed for generation.

Children’s Privacy

Walkout Intros is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly process data from anyone under that age.

Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Because we hold almost no data about you, there is very little for us to provide, correct, or delete. You fully control all content stored locally on your device. For any questions about the temporary AI processing, contact us and we’ll respond promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally (especially when we add analytics in the future). Any changes will be posted in the app and on this page with a new effective date. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
