Common Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I generate a walk-up intro?
    Tap the + button, enter player name/number, type or edit the script, choose an AI voice (Pro), pick a song from Apple Music or your library, then preview and save.
  • Free vs Pro?
    Free: Limited number of teams and players. One AI voice option.
    Pro: Unlimited teams, unlimited players per team, and the ability to choose from multiple AI voices. Both plans include a 1-week introductory offer.
  • Restore purchases?
    Go to Settings inside the app and tap “Restore Purchases”.
  • AI voice not generating?
    Make sure you have a stable internet connection (AI generation requires it). Try a shorter script or different voice.
  • Can I use my own custom scripts?
    Yes! You can fully edit the AI-generated script or write your own from scratch.
  • Lineup playback order?
    Create a lineup for a specific game, then play the intros in order — perfect for the dugout, sideline, or announcer booth.

Everything important stays on your device — teams, players, lineups, and saved intros. The only thing sent temporarily is the text for AI voice generation (deleted immediately after).
