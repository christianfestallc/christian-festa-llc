Common Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I turn on DNS filtering?
    Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management → DNS → Add Ghosty.
  • Free vs Premium?

  • Nothing is blocking?

    iOS 18+ only. Restart your iPhone and try again.
