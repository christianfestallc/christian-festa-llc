About Ghosty

Ghosty is a privacy-first iOS application developed by Christian Festa LLC. It filters unwanted connections on your device using custom domains you add (limited to 3 in the free tier, unlimited in premium) or curated blocklist categories you can toggle on/off.

Premium unlocks viewing of all traffic logs (free tier shows only blocked entries) and unlimited custom domains. All features run locally on your iPhone or iPad — zero data collection, zero servers.
